# Workflow: Competitive Intelligence Brief

A structured template for analyzing any competitor. Copy this into Claude.ai and fill in the bracketed fields.

## The Template

```
I'm building a competitive intelligence brief. Here is what I know
about the competitor:

Company: [COMPETITOR NAME]
Their positioning: [PASTE FROM THEIR WEBSITE — homepage headline, about page, or tagline]
Their product/features: [PASTE OR SUMMARIZE THEIR KEY FEATURES]
Their pricing: [IF KNOWN — pricing page info or tier names]
Their recent news: [PASTE ANY RECENT ANNOUNCEMENTS, PRESS, OR CHANGES]

My company: [YOUR COMPANY NAME]
Our positioning: [BRIEF SUMMARY OF WHO YOU ARE AND WHAT YOU DO]
Our key differentiators: [LIST 3-5 THINGS YOU DO BETTER OR DIFFERENTLY]

Please produce:
1. Executive Summary (3 sentences on who they are, what they do, and their market position)
2. Target Audience Analysis (who they sell to and how — inferred from their copy and pricing)
3. Messaging Analysis (key themes, value propositions, and tone of their marketing)
4. SWOT Analysis (formatted as a 2x2 table: Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, Threats)
5. Competitive Response Recommendations (3-5 specific actions we should take in our messaging or positioning)
6. Key Quotes (3-5 notable claims from their materials that we should be prepared to address in sales conversations)
```

## Filled Example Inputs

```
Company: ProjectBeam
Their positioning: "Where Teams Do Their Best Work — trusted by 50,000+
teams worldwide, bringing projects, tasks, and communication into one platform."
Their product/features: Kanban boards, Gantt charts, time tracking,
resource management, 200+ integrations, AI-powered workflows, mobile app.
Starting at $12/user/month.
Their recent news: Just launched AI-powered workflows feature in January.
Announced Series C funding of $80M.

My company: TaskFlow Pro
Our positioning: AI-powered project management that predicts and prevents
delays before they happen.
Our key differentiators:
1. AI resource allocation that predicts bottlenecks 2 weeks in advance
2. Automated weekly status reports (SmartReport)
3. 30% average reduction in project delays for customers
4. Setup in 5 minutes, no IT required
5. Purpose-built for mid-size teams (not trying to be everything for everyone)
```

## Tips

- **Update regularly.** Run this template whenever a competitor makes a major announcement, launches a feature, or changes pricing.
- **Paste actual copy**, not your memory of it. Go to their website and copy/paste — Claude can only analyze what you give it.
- **Combine with web search.** If you have web search enabled, you can ask Claude to search for recent news about the competitor to supplement what you've pasted.
- **Share the output.** The brief this produces is a useful artifact for sales enablement, product marketing, and leadership — save it and distribute it.
