# Workflow: Content Repurposing Engine

Turn one piece of content into multiple formats. Copy this template into Claude.ai whenever you need to distribute a piece across channels.

## The Template

```
Here is my source content:
[PASTE BLOG POST, WEBINAR TRANSCRIPT, WHITEPAPER SECTION, CASE STUDY, ETC.]

Please repurpose this into the following formats:

1. A LinkedIn post (150-200 words, conversational tone, ends with a question to drive engagement)

2. A tweet thread (5-7 tweets, each under 280 characters, first tweet is the hook)

3. An email newsletter blurb (100 words, includes a CTA to read the full piece at [URL])

4. 3 pull quotes suitable for social media graphics (short, punchy, under 20 words each)

Maintain the key message: [ONE SENTENCE SUMMARY OF THE CORE POINT]
Brand voice: [2-3 ADJECTIVES — e.g., "direct, conversational, confident"]
```

## Filled Example

```
Here is my source content:

"When DataCorp switched from manual project reporting to TaskFlow Pro's
AI-powered SmartReport, their Monday status meetings went from 60 minutes
to 15. Project lead Maria Chen said, 'We stopped talking about work and
started doing it.' Within 60 days, their team eliminated 4 hours of
weekly reporting overhead and caught two potential deadline risks before
they became problems. The marketing ops team alone saved 160 hours in
the first quarter — time they reinvested into campaign optimization
that drove a 15% improvement in lead quality."

Please repurpose this into the following formats:

1. A LinkedIn post (150-200 words, conversational tone, ends with a question to drive engagement)

2. A tweet thread (5-7 tweets, each under 280 characters, first tweet is the hook)

3. An email newsletter blurb (100 words, includes a CTA to read the full case study at https://example.com/datacore-case-study)

4. 3 pull quotes suitable for social media graphics (short, punchy, under 20 words each)

Maintain the key message: AI-powered reporting eliminates hours of manual work
and helps teams focus on high-impact activities.
Brand voice: direct, confident, results-oriented
```

## Tips

- **Start with your longest, most detailed piece** (blog post or case study). It's easier to compress down than to expand up.
- **Paste the actual content**, not a summary. Claude does a better job repurposing when it has the full original.
- **Customize the formats** for your channels. Replace "tweet thread" with "Instagram caption" or add "Slack announcement" — whatever channels you use.
- **Run it twice** with different angles if you want variety. Ask Claude to emphasize a different aspect of the source content each time.
