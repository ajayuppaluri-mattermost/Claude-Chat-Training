# Claude.ai Training Course

A 5-day hands-on training program for marketing and sales professionals to learn how to use Claude.ai effectively in day-to-day work.

## Who This Is For

Non-technical marketing and sales team members with access to a Claude.ai Team or Enterprise account. No prior AI experience required.

## What You'll Learn

| Day | Theme | Time |
|-----|-------|------|
| [Day 1](days/day-1-foundations/README.md) | **Foundations** — Navigate Claude, write your first prompts, get immediate results | 40 min |
| [Day 2](days/day-2-prompting/README.md) | **Prompting** — Get exactly what you need with specificity, context, and iteration | 43 min |
| [Day 3](days/day-3-writing/README.md) | **Writing Workflows** — Emails, blog posts, content repurposing, and editing | 44 min |
| [Day 4](days/day-4-research/README.md) | **Research Workflows** — Competitive analysis, data interpretation, and briefs | 43 min |
| [Day 5](days/day-5-advanced/README.md) | **Advanced & Team** — Projects, Artifacts, Cowork, Code, and team conventions | 45 min |

**Total time: ~3.5 hours across 5 days**

## Prerequisites

- A Claude.ai Team or Enterprise account (ask your admin if you don't have one)
- A web browser or the Claude desktop app
- 30-45 minutes of focused time each day

## How to Use This Course

1. Complete one day per day, in order — each day builds on the previous
2. Each day includes a **lesson** (how-to guide), **exercises** (hands-on practice), and a **workflow** (reusable template)
3. Do the exercises in Claude.ai as you read — this is learn-by-doing, not lecture
4. Save the workflow templates for daily use after the course
