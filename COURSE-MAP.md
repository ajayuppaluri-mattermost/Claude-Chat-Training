# Course Map

## Progression

```
Day 1: FOUNDATIONS           Day 2: PROMPTING            Day 3: WRITING
"I can talk to Claude"  →   "I get what I want"    →    "Claude writes for me daily"
                                                              ↓
                              Day 5: TEAM WORKFLOWS  ←   Day 4: RESEARCH
                              "We use Claude as a team"  "Claude does my analysis"
```
